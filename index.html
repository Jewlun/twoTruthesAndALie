Two Truthes and One Lie

Rules

"Two truths and a lie" is a game where players take turns sharing three statements about themselves, two of which are true and one of which is a lie.


Select the lie out of the three statements to gain points. If you got more than 9 questions right you must be spying on me! Good luck!
